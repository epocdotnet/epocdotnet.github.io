Welcome to Tuesday Knight Productions

Tuesday Knight Productions is an independent film studio that produces high-quality films that entertain and inspire audiences. We are committed to telling stories that matter, and we believe that film is a powerful medium that can change the world.

We are currently in production on our next film, "The Last Days of Summer." This film is a coming-of-age story about a young woman who is coming to terms with her own mortality. We are excited to share this film with the world, and we hope that it will resonate with audiences everywhere.

About Tuesday Knight Productions

Tuesday Knight Productions was founded in 2015 by Tuesday Knight, a filmmaker with over 20 years of experience in the industry. Tuesday is a passionate advocate for independent film, and she believes that independent filmmakers have the power to tell stories that would not be told otherwise.

Tuesday Knight Productions is committed to producing films that are both entertaining and thought-provoking. We believe that film is a powerful medium that can change the world, and we are excited to use our platform to tell stories that matter.
